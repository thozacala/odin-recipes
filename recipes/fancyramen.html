Fancy Ramen

Description

One bite of this Fancy Ramen and you will taste exactly what you expect from yourself. That's right! Add whatever you feel like adding to the dish, and it will be exactly what you want!

Not to be mistaken for a delicious dish, this ramen will knock your socks off. In fact, if you want to put your socks themselves into the dish, feel free!

Ingredients

  • Cheap Ramen Noodles
  • Literally Anything
  • Your Socks(optional)
  • Water
  • Tons of Salt

Steps

  1. Cook your cheap ramen in a microwave
  2. Add All your ingredients
  3. Garnish with your socks
  4. Enjoy!
