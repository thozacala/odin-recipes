Unicorn Spaghetti

Description

This spaghetti is not your mom's. It is unicorn spaghetti. Not much more to say about it. It is a wonderful spaghetti, and can't be beaten when eaten. Fruity and salty all the same, It's a real treat.

This spaghetti will be all over your sweater, and will double as a pride dish for all to enjoy

Ingredients

  • Rainbow Noodles
  • Unicorn Blood
  • Tears of your Enemies

Steps

  1. Cook the Rainbow Noodles using preferred method
  2. Add the Unicorn Blood
  3. Add the Tears of your Enemies
  4. Enjoy!
